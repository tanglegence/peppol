We hereby publish a ‘Call for Candidates’ for the following OpenPeppol governance positions open in 2021:

 

A. Positions to be elected by the 13th General Assembly

 

  1. Secretary General
  2. Managing Committee
    • 1 representative of Peppol Authorities
    • 1 representative of Service Providers
    • 2 representatives of End Users
  3. Election Committee
    • 1 representative of Peppol Authorities
    • 1 representative of Service Providers
    • 1 representative of End Users

 

B. Positions to be elected by the Communities

 

  1. Peppol Authority Community
    • Peppol Authority Community Leader
  2. Service Provider Community
    • Service Provider Community Leader
  3. End User Community
    • End User Community Leader
  4. eDelivery Community
    • 2 members of the eDEC Change Management Board
  5. Post-Award Community
    • Post-Award Community Leader
    • 2 members of the PoAC Change Management Board
  6. Pre-Award Community
    • 2 members of the PrAC Change Management Board
  7. Agreements, Policies and Procedures Change Management Board (APP CMB): New body, read the Call for Candidates document for details
    • 2 representatives of Peppol Authorities
    • 2 representatives of Service Providers

 

The deadline for submitting candidacies is May 7th, 2021.

 

Please read the detailed Call for Candidates document at:

2021 Call for Candidates in OpenPeppol Elections_2021.04.16_PUBLISHED

 

We strongly encourage all members who have both the interest and motivation, to consider putting forward candidates for any of the open positions.
